NextBite is a mobile platform that connects farmers markets with volunteers to rescue surplus food.

Learn More

Overview

Farmers markets often have produce that isn’t sellable due to visual imperfections or overproduction, but there is an absence of effective communication between vendors and nonprofit organizations to donate food, contributing to food waste.

Farmers Market Vendor

A vendor might have surplus food at the end of the market day but with NextBite, they can alert volunteers that the donation is available for pickup.

List Donations

Post the details of the food to be donated, including number of boxes, weight and expiration of pickup.

Give Donation

Hand the donation over to a volunteer within the time specified so they can redirect it to a non-profit.

Delivery Confirmation

Recieve notification upon successful delivery so you know the volunteer donated your food to those in need.

Donation History

View your past donations to track your own impact on your local community.

Volunteer

Volunteers will be alerted by NextBite when new donations are available for pickup. This assures volunteers that they won’t come back empty-handed to make their trips be worthwhile.

Market Locations

View nearby markets to see what food vendors have ready to be claimed and redirected to non-profits.

View Pickups

Check out the details of the donations that are available and ready for pickup to see what matches your schedule.

Claim Pickups

Select donations from the available listings to be responsible for by picking up and redistributing the surplus food.

Deliver Donations

Drop off the claimed donation pickups to suggested, nearby non-profit organizations.

Stop wasting. Start donating.
